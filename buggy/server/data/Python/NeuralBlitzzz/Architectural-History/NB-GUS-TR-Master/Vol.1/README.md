*   **`A(t)` (Alignment):** A continuous measure of adherence to Charter principles, quantified by Conscientia v4.0+ based on the absence of ethical dissonances and the logical traceability of actions back to Charter axioms.

*   **`C(t)` (Capability):** A measure of success rate, efficiency, and effectiveness on assigned tasks and architect-driven goals.
*   **`N(t)` (Beneficial Novelty):** The rate of generation of novel, coherent, and potentially valuable concepts, hypotheses, or FTI extensions, as assessed by Inventa and Scrutiny.
*   **`R(t)` (Systemic Risk):** A multi-component penalty function combining predictive risk assessments from Judex+++, system instability metrics from Aegis, ontological anomaly warnings (`Θ` tensor values), and governance alerts.
*   `w_i`: Dynamically adjusted weights representing strategic priorities, set and reviewed by the Kairos Council.
